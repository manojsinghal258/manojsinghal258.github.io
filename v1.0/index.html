My Journey

With 15+ years of extensive experience leading Quality Assurance strategies, I currently serve as the Lead Software Quality Assurance Engineer at Opaque Systems in the San Francisco Bay Area. My academic foundation was built at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IIT Delhi).

Recognized as an outstanding researcher and engineer, I was awarded the US EB-1A visa, an honor reserved for individuals with extraordinary ability. As a Senior Member of the IEEE, I am actively engaged in advancing tech standards, reviewing research, and publishing papers on Cloud infrastructure, Backup/Recovery, and AI-driven testing.

Beyond my professional role, I am committed to making this world a better place to live—a philosophy that drives my mentorship, speaking engagements, and written articles navigating the startup ecosystem and AI engineering.
